Market

Android Market

  • there are > 400k applications
  • 10 billion downloads
  • very low entry : cost 25 $ to sign it for the market
  • if you have an app ready it takes a few minutes to deploy an app

What is important for your app?

  • you need visibility
  • ponder carefully the content of the market description
  • use same seo techniques to improve search visibility
  • translate at least the market description!
  • make good icons

Before deploying you app?

  • sign your apk
  • use zipalign tool
  • add bugsense/acra
  • add analytics
  • if your granpa doesn't understand your app add a tutorial

After

  • check carefully exception and fix quickly
  • always read comments
  • write blogs about your app

Exceptions and ANR

  • management of exception is not very good in the market
  • use acra/bugsense
